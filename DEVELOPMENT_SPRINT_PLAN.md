Version: 1.0

Development Model: MVP First

Launch a functional production-ready marketplace with lead generation, listings, admin dashboard, and payments.

# DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY

DO NOT BUILD:

* Buildertrend Clone
* IndiaMART Clone
* Houzz Clone
* Urban Company Clone

Build:

A lightweight marketplace inspired by those platforms.

Focus:

* Listings
* Leads
* Profiles
* Search
* Payments

Everything else later.
