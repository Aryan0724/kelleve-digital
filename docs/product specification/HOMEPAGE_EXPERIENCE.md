# FIND MY INTERIOR - HOMEPAGE EXPERIENCE SPECIFICATION

## Purpose

The homepage is the first impression of the platform.

It should immediately answer one question:

**"What can I do here?"**

The homepage should never feel like a static directory.

It should feel alive.

It should constantly show opportunities, recommendations and actions relevant to the logged in user.

The homepage should become different depending on who is logged in.

---

# GLOBAL PRINCIPLES

Every homepage must contain

• Primary Action

• Personalized Recommendations

• Recent Activity

• Opportunities

• Messages Preview

• Notifications

• Featured Professionals

• Search

Every section must have a purpose.

Remove every decorative section that has no business value.

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

Every user should understand what to do within five seconds of opening the homepage.

Every section should increase engagement.

Every click should lead to a working feature.

The homepage should feel like the operating system of the user's business instead of a marketing landing page.
